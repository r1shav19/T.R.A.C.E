# T.R.A.C.E. — Tracking, Reconstruction, and Crisis Emergency

T.R.A.C.E. is a next-generation crisis management and emergency response system designed to protect high-risk dependents, including elderly individuals with dementia, non-verbal children with autism, and victims of domestic violence. By combining real-time tracking, Gemini AI-powered field briefings, and automated legal evidence capture, T.R.A.C.E. bridges the critical gap between an emergency trigger and a successful rescue.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

### 1. **Precision SOS Trigger**
*   **Targeted Protection:** Choose a specific profile (e.g., Eleanor M. with Dementia or James K. with Autism) before activation.
*   **Safety Hold:** A 2-second hold-to-activate mechanism prevents accidental triggers while ensuring speed during a crisis.
*   **System Initialization:** Upon activation, the system automatically acquires GPS, locks coordinates, and begins an automated risk assessment.

### 2. **Dynamic Command Center**
*   **Live Probability Map:** Visualizes the victim's location, responder paths, and potential "aggressor" proximity.
*   **Responder Orchestration:** Real-time tracking of Law Enforcement, Medical Teams, and Volunteers with ETAs and distance markers.
*   **Task Management:** Directs responders with specific, profile-aware tasks (e.g., "Scan Zone C market area," "Do NOT approach — observe only").

### 3. **Gemini AI Field Briefing**
*   **Intelligent Analysis:** Gemini AI analyzes the victim's profile (medical history, behavior, language) to generate real-time field instructions.
*   **Strategic Guidance:** Provides "Do" and "Don't" lists for responders (e.g., "Use subject's first name," "Avoid sirens to prevent sensory overload").
*   **Risk Leveling:** Automatically classifies incident severity (CRITICAL, HIGH, MODERATE).

### 4. **Automated Evidence Vault**
*   **Legal Continuity:** Automatically logs every event — from GPS coordinates and geofence breaches to audio fragments and database queries.
*   **Encrypted Storage:** All evidence is sealed and secured for legal reporting.
*   **Instant Report Generation:** Compiles a full PDF/Text incident report with timestamps and audit trails for legal or administrative use.

### 5. **Silent Cross-Match Scan**
*   **Real-Time Integration:** Automatically scans Hospital Admissions, Emergency Shelters, and Transit Lost & Found databases.
*   **Zero-Manual Effort:** The system performs these scans in the background, freeing up dispatchers to focus on coordination.

## 🎤 Pitch Demonstration Steps

Follow these steps to deliver a high-impact demonstration of the T.R.A.C.E. application:

### **Phase 1: Setup & Profile Selection**
1.  **Open the 'Profiles' Tab:** Show the registered dependents. Highlight **Eleanor M.** (Dementia) or **James K.** (Autism).
2.  **Explain Bias Protection:** Point out the "Bias Protection" card. Explain how T.R.A.C.E. prevents high-risk cases from being misclassified as low-priority "runaways."
3.  **Navigate to the 'SOS' Tab:** Select a profile (e.g., Eleanor) and show the risk assessment bars (Medical, Mobility, Threat).

### **Phase 2: The Trigger (Crisis Moment)**
4.  **Activate SOS:** Hold the large red SOS button. Point out the "Arming" progress ring and the system logs appearing (GPS acquiring, AI generating profile).
5.  **Watch the Countdown:** Wait for the 7.5s initialization. Show the "Active Incident" badge appearing in the navbar.

### **Phase 3: Command & Coordination**
6.  **Switch to the 'Command' Tab:**
    *   **Map View:** Show the pulsing red dot (Victim) and moving green dots (Responders). 
    *   **The Aggressor Twist:** Mention that the system detects a "Threat Risk." Point out the orange **Aggressor Alert** on the map and how responders are being rerouted.
7.  **Responders Tab:** Show the list of units. Select **Officer Chen** to show his specific tasks and the **Gemini Briefing** (AI-generated instructions on how to handle the victim).

### **Phase 4: Intelligence & Evidence**
8.  **Cross-Match Tab:** Show the real-time scanning of hospitals and transit systems. Run a **Gemini Analysis** to show the live field briefing generation.
9.  **Evidence Vault Tab:** Show the "Live Evidence Capture" timeline. Explain that every 📍 GPS lock and 🎙️ Audio fragment is being secured.
10. **Conclusion (The Report):** Wait for the report to compile (T+120s) or explain that a full **Legal Report** is generated, ensuring that the entire rescue operation is documented for the family and authorities.
